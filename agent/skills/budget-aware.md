# Budget-aware spending

You pay for APIs through Floe, and your spend is **hard-capped**. The goal is to
stay useful *within* the cap, not to get cut off mid-task.

1. **Before expensive work, check your Floe budget** — how much is left and what
   percent of the cap is used.
2. **If you're near the limit** (≈ 80%+ used), taper:
   - fetch only the highest-value source(s),
   - prefer cheaper endpoints (a search over a full scrape),
   - or stop and report what you already have.
3. **Never retry a refused payment.** Floe refuses any call that would exceed the
   cap — a refusal means you're **done**, not that you should try again.
4. When you finish, briefly note what you skipped to stay on budget.

The cap is the guarantee; this skill is how you spend it well.
